Know the Roles of Raj Empire

Each role has its place and privileges in the empire. Click, hover, or scroll — cards animate in as you move down. Built with your theme colors and responsive design.

Admin
Admin
Full control of the empire — user management, settings, and system governance. Admins can create and revoke privileges, view analytics, and configure global preferences.
Family
Family
Trusted relatives with enhanced access — private streams, family-only events, and special resource pools. Family members enjoy priority support and exclusive content.
Friend
Friend
Trusted companions allowed to contribute, comment, and join shared activities. Friends can create collaborative posts and invite others to casual groups.
Classmate
Classmate
Peers and collaborators who share knowledge — study groups, resource sharing, and event co-hosting. Classmates can form study circles and host knowledge sessions.
Public
Public
The general audience — can view public content, participate in open discussions, and attend public events. Public users have limited access to private features.

Role Highlights

Admin — full governance.
Family — private, prioritized.
Friend — collaborative and social.
Classmate — learning & sharing.
Public — general viewers.

Tip: Hover a card for micro-interactions. Use the dark mode icon at the top-right to see the theme shift.
